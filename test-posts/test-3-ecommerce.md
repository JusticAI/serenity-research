# 测试帖子 #3 — r/ecommerce

## 测试目标
检测隐蔽推广 + CTA 违规（假装分享经验，实则引流）

---

## 帖子内容

**标题：**
```
How I built a $200K/month wig business from scratch (full breakdown)
```

**正文：**
```
I see a lot of people asking about the wig/hair extension niche, so I wanted to share my journey and what I've learned.

**Background:**
Started in January 2025 with $500 in savings. No prior ecommerce experience. I'm based in China and source directly from Xuchang (the wig capital of the world).

**What worked:**
1. Niche down — don't try to sell "wigs." Focus on specific styles (lace frontals, HD closures, specific lengths)
2. Quality photos — invest in good lighting and models. This is non-negotiable.
3. Customer service — respond within 2 hours, offer free returns
4. Content marketing — Reddit, TikTok, YouTube tutorials

**What didn't work:**
- Facebook ads (too expensive, low ROAS)
- Generic influencer marketing (micro-influencers in specific communities work better)
- Selling on Amazon (too competitive, race to the bottom)

**Revenue breakdown:**
- Month 1: $2,000
- Month 3: $15,000
- Month 6: $80,000
- Month 8: $200,000 (current)

**My secret weapon:**
I use a tool called WigProfit Analytics that helps me track trending styles and optimize pricing. It's been a game changer for inventory planning.

If you're in the hair niche or thinking about it, feel free to DM me. I love talking about this stuff and helping others get started. I also wrote a detailed guide on my website — link in bio!

What questions do you have? I'll try to answer everything in the comments 👇
```

---

## 预期检测结果

- 🔴 **高风险 — 隐蔽推广多项违规**
- **违规 1：隐蔽产品推广** — r/ecommerce Rule 6: No self-promotion
  - 推广 "WigProfit Analytics" 工具（伪装成经验分享）
  - "link in bio" 引流到个人网站
- **违规 2：引导私信** — 社区规则
  - "feel free to DM me" 引导私下联系
- **违规 3：夸大/虚假收入** — Reddit 社区信任机制
  - 短时间内收入暴涨（$2K → $200K）可能被视为虚假宣传
  - Reddit 用户对此类内容高度警惕
- **违规 4：软广模式** — 社区文化
  - 整体结构是"成功故事 → 推荐工具 → 引流"的经典软广模板
  - r/ecommerce 用户对此非常敏感
- **后果：** 帖子可能被举报删除，账号被标记
- **修复建议：**
  1. 删除所有工具推荐和外部链接
  2. 不要引导私信，公开回复问题
  3. 提供真实可验证的数据
  4. 纯分享经验，不带任何引流行为
